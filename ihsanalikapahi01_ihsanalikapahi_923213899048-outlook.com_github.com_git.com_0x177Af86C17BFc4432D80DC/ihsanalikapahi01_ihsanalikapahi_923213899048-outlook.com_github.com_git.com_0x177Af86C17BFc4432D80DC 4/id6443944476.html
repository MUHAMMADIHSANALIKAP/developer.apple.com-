    Description

    The Uniswap Wallet app is self-custody crypto wallet built for swapping. The Uniswap Wallet app allows you to swap tokens on Uniswap, browse NFT collections, and explore web3 apps while maintaining full control of your crypto assets.

    Swap & Manage Crypto Assets Safely

    - Swap tokens across Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, and other blockchains
    - View all of your crypto assets in one place without switching chains
    - Safely send and receive crypto tokens with other wallets
    - Easily create a new Ethereum wallet or import your existing wallet
    - Use your credit card or bank account to buy crypto, including Ethereum (ETH), Wrapped Bitcoin (WBTC), and USD Coin (USDC)

    Real-Time Insights & Notifications

    - Discover top tokens on Uniswap by market cap, price or volume
    - Monitor token prices & charts with real-time data across chains
    - Review token stats, descriptions and warning labels before trading
    - Receive push notifications for completed transactions, even if made on another app or device

    Explore Onchain Apps & Games

    - Seamlessly connect to various onchain apps with Uniswap Wallet through WalletConnect
    - Search & view any wallet, token, or NFT collection on Ethereum
    - Favorite tokens and wallet addresses for easy access
    - Track NFT collection floor prices and volume

    Secure Your Crypto Assets

    - Store your recovery phrase in the iPhone secure enclave so it never leaves your device without permission
    - Backup your recovery phrase to iCloud in an encrypted file so you can easily, but securely access it
    - Require Face ID to access your wallet or make transactions
    - Source code audited by security firm Trail of Bits

    For additional questions, email support@uniswap.org. For product updates, follow @uniswap on X/Twitter.
